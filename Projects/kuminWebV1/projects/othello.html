Othello

Othello is a two player game where they place either a White or a Black piece on the board and flip an enemy's (opposing players) piece. The winner is determined by the greater piece number on the board.

This project was programmed in C++ during my third semester of my college. This project helped me learn more about C++ on memory management, pointers, vectors, and the MVC software design pattern.
